Rollinia

Tropical South America's most aromatic fruit

Rollinia Deliciosa · Tropical South America

Rare Variety
Flavor Profile

Mildly acidic and intensely aromatic, Rollinia (also called Biriba or Wild Sweetsop) delivers a tropical medley — pineapple, banana, and coconut in every bite. The large conical fruit turns bright yellow when ripe, revealing white translucent jelly-like flesh. Best scooped fresh with a spoon straight from the skin.

Health Benefits
  • Rich in dietary fiber — supports healthy digestion
  • High in Vitamin C, Calcium, Phosphorus, Iron and Protein
  • Contains essential amino acids: Lysine, Methionine, Threonine, Tryptophan
  • Natural antioxidants help reduce oxidative stress
Cultivation & Care
  • Fruits within 2–3 years from planting — relatively fast bearer
  • Moderate maintenance; prefers warm, humid tropical conditions
  • Harvest only when fully ripe and soft — slice into wedges and scoop pulp
  • Small dark brown seeds are not edible — discard before eating
  • Great fresh, in smoothies, desserts, juices and tropical blends
